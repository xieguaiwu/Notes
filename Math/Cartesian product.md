# Cartesian product
$$\mathbf{A}\times\mathbf{B}=\{x\in {\mathcal {P}}({\mathcal {P}}(A\cup B))\mid \exists a\in A\ \exists b\in B:x=(a,b)\}$$
$$\text{where $x\in\mathcal{P}(\mathcal{P}(\mathbf{A}\cup\mathbf{B}))=x\subseteq(\mathbf{A}\cup\mathbf{B})$}$$
